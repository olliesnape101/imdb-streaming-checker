How to Export IMDb Lists as CSV

Follow these steps to export any IMDb list (Watchlist, custom list, ratings, etc.):

  1. Head to IMDb in a browser and open any of your IMDb lists (e.g., your Watchlist).
    • Make sure your list contains films/TV shows - it does not work for lists of people, for instance.
    • Find your Watchlist by simply clicking on the Watchlist icon in the top right. If this icon isn't there, click on your profile name dropdown in the top right, then Your Watchlist.
    • For any of your other lists, find them by clicking on your profile name dropdown in the top right, then Your lists.
  2. On your list page, Select ⭳ Export in the top right.
  3. You should now be prompted to Open exports page. Simply select this.
    • If you don't see this prompt, simply scroll down on the page and you should see Your exports > in the right-hand column or at the bottom of the page - simply click on this.
      • If you don't see Your exports > either, scroll back up and click ⭳ Export again.
  4. On your exports page, simply click the Ready ⭳ icon next to your desired list, and choose a place to dowload this CSV file if prompted.

Once you have downloaded your CSV file, return to the main page to upload it.
